About Our Matrimony Service

Connecting hearts since 2010 with trust and tradition

Our Story

Founded in 2010, our matrimony service began with a simple mission: to help people find their life partners through a trusted, community-oriented platform. What started as a small local service in Kilinochchi has now grown to serve thousands of families across Northern Sri Lanka.

We specialize in both traditional arranged marriages and second marriages, respecting all cultural and religious backgrounds. Our team personally verifies every profile to ensure authenticity and quality matches.
